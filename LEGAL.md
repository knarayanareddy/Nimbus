## 1. Important Disclaimer

**ClimaFi is a decentralized parametric risk protocol built on Solana.**

This software is provided **"AS IS"** without warranty of any kind. The protocol enables users to purchase parametric coverage based on objective weather data (rainfall indices). It is **not traditional insurance**.

**Users should understand the following:**

- Parametric products pay based on **pre-defined triggers**, not actual loss.
- There is **no guarantee** of payout even if the user experiences weather-related loss.
- The protocol is experimental and carries **significant financial and technical risk**.
